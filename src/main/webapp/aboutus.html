About Findex

Financial Indexing for Empowerment and Experiencing

At Findex (Financial Indexing for Empowerment and Experiencing), we believe that every Filipino deserves the tools to make smarter financial decisions and build a secure future. We are a platform that connects citizens with the government aid they are eligible for, while also guiding them toward the most suitable BPI savings and insurance products.

Our mission is to make financial empowerment simple, accessible, and personalized. Whether you're a student saving for tuition, a parent securing your child's future, or a farmer preparing for the next harvest season, Findex makes it easier to know your options, take action, and plan ahead.

Through our chatbot-powered guide, you can scan your ID to prefill details, instantly see which aid and benefits you qualify for, and receive customized savings and insurance recommendations. We even provide long-term financial simulations, showing how consistent saving today can transform into real protection and opportunities tomorrow. With just one valid ID, you can also open a BPI account in as fast as 2 minutes and unlock benefits such as 1-year free health insurance.

We are guided by five core values: Empowerment, Accessibility, Innovation, Trust, and Resilience. These principles ensure that Findex is more than just a tracker ‚Äî it's your partner in building stability and opportunity for yourself and your family.
